## Part I: The Null Hypothesis and Baseline Definitions

## Part III: Lunar Anomalies and Mathematical Improbabilities

The most glaring astrophysical anomaly in the entire measurable universe is locked in orbit around us. 

The Moon is a statistical impossibility. Standard planetary science utilizes the Giant Impact Hypothesis: a Mars-sized planetoidal object named Theia smashed into the proto-Earth 4.5 billion years ago, and the ejected debris accreted into the Moon. 

But the data does not reconcile.

Second, consider the orbital mathematics. The Sun is exactly 400 times larger than the Moon. The Sun is also exactly 400 times further away from the Earth than the Moon. This highly specific geometric ratio results in perfect total solar eclipses—an aesthetic and mathematical synchronicity that exists nowhere else in the observed solar system.
